Molten Iron Planet:e Rain You Can’t Imagin

If you thought rain on Earth was odd, get ready to be amazed by what happens on WASP-76b. On this scorching hot planet, temperatures reach over 2400°C, and that’s not all. At this temperature, it’s not water that evaporates but iron! Yes, iron evaporates into vapor in the sky, then condenses again to fall back to the surface as molten iron droplets. It’s a surreal image of a distant world where the sky rains burning metal instead of water!
